About Us

Dr. Alexander Tessler (PhD in Applied and Computational Mechanics, UCLA, 1979) has been employed by the U.S. aircraft industry, Army research, and NASA Langley Research Center (1991-2017). He has also served as adjunct professor at Northeastern University, Old Dominion University, George Washington University, and University of Virginia. In 2019, he was a visiting professor at Polytecnico di Torino, Italy.
